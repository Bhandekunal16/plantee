About us

There are an estimated 390,000 species of vascular plants on Earth, with about 10 percent more yet to be discovered. These plants, both known and unknown, may hold answers to many of the world's health, social, environmental, and economic problems. A comprehensive inventory of plant life is vital for protecting many threatened species and realizing their full potential before they, and the possibilities they offer, become extinct.

What you do with us

Our mission is to provide comprehensive information on all vascular plants on Earth. By joining us, you can help improve our database and contribute additional valuable information. Together, we aim to support efforts in understanding and conserving plant diversity, discovering new species, and exploring their potential to address global health, social, environmental, and economic challenges. Let's work together to protect and realize the full potential of the world's plant life before it's too late.
